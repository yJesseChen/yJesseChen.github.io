About me

  • I am currently a second year Ph.D. student of Applied and Computational Mathematics, advised by Prof. Dongbin Xiu.

  • My research interests fall in the broad areas of scientific computing and numerical analysis. Recently, I'm working on the machine learning methods for scientific computing, specifically the data-driven modeling and uncertainty quantification of physical systems governed by (stochastic) differential equations.

  • During the past few years, I'm grateful to work with mentors who have great influence on me. I am also interested in and working on discontinuous Galerkin (dG) method with Prof. Yulong Xing and Finite Element Method (FEM) with Prof. Xu Zhang and Prof. Songming Hou.

  • Please see my Research page, Google Scholar and ResearchGate for more information about my research.
