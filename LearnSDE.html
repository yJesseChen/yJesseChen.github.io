Learning Stochastic Dynamics

Double well potential

We consider a classical double-well system:

\[\begin{equation} d x_t = (x_t-x_t^3) d t+\sigma d W_{t} \end{equation} \]

Here \(\sigma\) is the pertubation constant. The following video shows the training data up to termination time \(T=1\). Note that almost no transition of states happen in this short time lag.

Upon learning the stochastic flow map, we could obtain accurate simulation up to \(T=100\) and predict transition of states without observing from training data.
