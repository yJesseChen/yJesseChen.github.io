Research

Research Interests

  • Data-driven modeling of systems driven by (stochastic) differential equations

  • Numerical simulation of stochastic differential equations and rare events

  • Finite Element Method, discontinuous Galerkin method, Virtual Element Method

  • Interface problems and coupling mathematical models arising from applications
